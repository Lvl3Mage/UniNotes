$f(x) = \ln(x)$ at a = 1
$P_{2,1} f(x) = f(1) + f'(1)(x-1) + \frac{f''(1))}{2!}(x-1)^2$

$f(x) = \ln x \to f(1) = \ln 1 = 0$
$f'(x) = \frac{1}{x} \to f(1) = \frac{1}{1} = 1$
$f''(x) = \frac{-1}{x^2} \to f(1) = \frac{-1}{1} = -1$

Thus
$$P_{2,1}f(x) = 1(x-1)-\frac{1}{2}(x-1)^2$$
